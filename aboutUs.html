About Me

Hi! I'm Irene Yu, a first year graduate sutudent at UMSI on UX track. I love traveling—not just for the adventure, but also for preserving the memories that come with it. Cultural derivatives and souvenirs help capture those moments and allow me to share them with family and friends.

This summer, I helped my sister at her design studio, which specializes in designing city-inspired cultural derivative and souvenirs. I realized their beautiful work needed a space to shine online, so I created this page to showcase their designs. I hope it brings you the same joy and inspiration that traveling brings to me.

Team

Welcome to Our Design Studio We are a passionate group of travelers and culture enthusiasts, dedicated to keeping the beauty of each journey alive. Inspired by traditional culture, iconic attractions, and art, we create designs that capture the essence of travel, preserving memories long after the journey ends. Our mission is to make travel experiences more meaningful by blending cultural elements into unique products that evoke lasting memories. Currently, our products are available in offline shops across Henan, Tianjin, Zhejiang, and many other cities. We hope our creations make your travel memories even more profound and help you cherish the beauty of each adventure.
